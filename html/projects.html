V.E.G.A.S

Completed Jan 2016 | IEEE at UCLA Open Project Space

Description:

V.E.G.A.S simulates a simple game similar to the casino games you might see in Las Vegas. Powered by a Teensy LC microcontroller, the three RGB LED's cycle through the colors red, blue, and green. Pressing the button in the middle stops the first LED from cycling and emits the color it stopped at. Pressing the button afterwards would similarly stop the second and the third LED's. The goal of the game is to get the three LED's to emit the same color. The three LED's then blink green (success) or red (fail) until the button is pressed again.
