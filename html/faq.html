Why do some people call you BJ?

It's my nickname.

I used to play badminton with my friend Jeffrey Chen and our coach needed a way to differentiate us since we have the same name. Our coach was going to call us Jeffrey 1 and Jeffrey 2 until our friend Stephen pointed at me and said:

"Why don't we call this one Big Jeffrey or BJ for short?"

The rest is history.
